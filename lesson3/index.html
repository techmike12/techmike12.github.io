Welcome to WeatherNews.live site plan.

Domain

I was able to find WeatherFacts.app from Google Domains. This was was priced at $20/year.

Site Purpose

This site will give users the ability to quickly glance at the weather and be right on there way. It is in fact simple and to the point. This gives users more time to think about what they want to do with the weather already in mind. If they need more information they can quickly toggle a button to show it. Though overall the site is meant to be a quick source of general weather information as to not clutter the users mind with unnecessary or unwanted information.
